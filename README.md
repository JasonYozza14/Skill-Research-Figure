## 💡 How to Make a Flowchart

1. Click **New Flowchart** on the main screen.  
2. Drag shapes from the side panel onto your empty workspace.  
3. Click inside a shape and type the text you want to appear.  
4. Use arrows to link the shapes. Click an arrow icon, then click the two shapes to connect.  
5. Move shapes around to adjust the layout until it looks clear.  
6. Save your work from the **File** menu any time.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting

If the app does not open or crashes:

- Make sure your Windows version is up to date.  
- Try running the app as an administrator (right-click the icon and select **Run as administrator**).  
- Restart your computer and try again.  
- Check if your antivirus software blocked the app and add it to exceptions if needed.

If your flowchart shapes or text do not appear correctly:

- Check that your graphics drivers are current.  
- Restart Skill-Research-Figure.
